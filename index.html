Unspoken Letter

To the person reading this, I just want to say I'm so grateful for you👊, and if it’s your first time reading this, welcome!👋 First of all, thank you for give me a chance to take this promise. I want you to know that I'm so grateful for your willingness to give me a chance😌. It means the world to me, and it's honestly made me feel like a new person. I'm more motivated than ever to be the best version of myself👨‍💻, because I know that's what you deserve🙏.

To be honest, I've never been the type of person to put my heart on the line. But you're different. You're someone I'm willing to fight for🙂, and I'm not going to let this chance pass me by. I know it won't be easy, but waiting for you is something I'm more than happy to do🤲.

I'm incredibly happy to share this website with you, which I've been working on for the past week😪. It's called "Story in the Clouds" and it's a virtual space for us to connect and share our lives, even when we're physically apart. I know we're both about to head back to our separate universities👩‍🎓, and the thought of being so far away from you fills me with a mixture of excitement and sadness. But this website is my way of saying that even though we'll be miles apart, we can always be close in our virtual space🕶.

You don't have to use this website every day, every week or be online every time✋. The important thing is that this website is here, waiting for you whenever you need it. It's a place where we can share our thoughts, feelings, and experiences, without any judgment or pressure💡. This website is my way of staying connected with you, even when we're apart. Let this website be a virtual version of me🕴.

Epilogue : Ed Sheeran's 'Perfect' was the soundtrack to my coding sessions when I built this website🎵. It always makes me emotional, because it reminds me of how lucky I am to have found someone as special as you🤝. If you're ever exploring this website, put on 'Perfect' and see if you can feel the same magic I did while creating it. Thank you again for being you😸. I can't wait to see what the future holds for us✨.

This is probably the best feature for me on this website 🤩. I believe we can do a lot of things by creating a virtual room with Figjam 👨‍🏫. I've designed some decorations for the virtual room 👓, including a board for sharing information about academic activities 👩‍🎓 and campus life, such as scholarships, competitions, internships, etc 🏂. Of course, there's always room for real-time conversations 🦻 where we can connect virtually. I'll also create some other sharing spaces, such as a chat area 📣, a recommendation list, life updates, and even shitposting 🤸‍♂️.
If you want to talk to me but too afraid to send me a message 😥, you can use this feature 🙌. It will take you to my virtual Gmail 🦸‍♂️, where you can write anything you want. You can share life updates, tell me a story ✍, give me film n music recommendations or even invite me to hang out. Just click the image on the right to send ur letter 👉🏃‍♂️
You probably already know about this playlist I've created. Thank you so much for listening, it means a lot to me 🤗. Just wanted u to know that I'll be adding new songs in the future. If you want to be a collaborator on this playlist, pls just let me know :)
